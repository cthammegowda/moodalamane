ABOUT US


At Moodalamane we have a culture of loving and living with the nature. The food, the coffee, the honey and the spices we use are procured locally and some of these directly from 70-acre estate moodalamane is part of. While luxury can be enticing in a place like chikamagalur, nothing beats waking up right in the middle of a lush green coffee plantation with birds chirping and looming mountains as your morning company!
Our homestay offers discerning visitors the unique opportunity of experiencing life close to nature, but with the privacy and level of comfort afforded by the best hotels. Moodalamane is the perfect choice for birdwatchers and nature lovers looking for a relaxed holiday in tranquil surroundings.

ATTRACTIONS NEARBY

While moodalamane offers unique experiences with a host of unexplored hikes and bird watching spots within the lush green estate, there are world heritage sites and breath taking western ghats within convinient driving distance from our estate.

Historical Temples/WHO sites
Belur, halebeedu, Dharmastala, sringeri, horanadu, Subramanya, Kalasa

Scenic drives and water bodies
Charmadi ghat, kemmangundi, kuduremuk, Bhadra reservoir, Mani dam

National Parks/Wildlife Sanctuaries
Kuduremuk national park, Bhadra wildlife sanctuary, Pushpagiri wildlife sanctuary & someshware sanctuary

Mudigere is 250 kms from Bangalore (Bangalore - Hassan - Belur - Mudigere)
